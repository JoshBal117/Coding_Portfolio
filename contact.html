Who I am, and what I do.

Hi, I’m Joshua Balao — a Full Stack Web Developer with a heart for purposeful technology and a passion for clean, scalable code. After completing the Full Stack Web Development program at the University of Central Florida, I built a portfolio of fully functional applications that reflect real-world challenges and collaborative problem-solving. Whether solo or team-based, every project has given me hands-on experience in full-cycle development — from ideation and design to deployment and iteration.

Outside of tech, I’m a dedicated husband and father of two. My family is the driving force behind my work ethic and attention to detail. They’ve taught me the value of patience, perseverance, and building things that last — values I bring to every line of code.

This portfolio reflects not just what I’ve built, but who I strive to be: a reliable developer, a committed teammate, and a person who believes that great software should serve people first. Many of these projects are still growing — just like I am
